We do not track users without permission. The app may use analytics tools to improve stability and user experience.

We use industry-standard encryption, secure servers, and strict access controls to protect your personal data from unauthorized access or misuse.

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal information. Only aggregated, non-identifiable usage data may be shared for analytics and performance improvements.

Legal Compliance

We may disclose information only if required by law or to protect user safety, prevent fraud, or safeguard the rights of our users.

Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your Matrix account from within the app through:

  • Open MATRIX App
  • Go to “Security Settings”
  • Select “Delete Account”
  • Confirm and complete the deletion

Once deleted, all your user data will be permanently removed from our system.
