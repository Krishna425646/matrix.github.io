Privacy Policy – MATRIX

Welcome to MATRIX! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal and non-personal information when you use the Matrix mobile application and its features. By using Matrix, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use the app:

  • Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Email Address
  • Device Information (model, OS version)
  • App Usage Data & Crash Logs
  • Wallet and Transaction Details (inside the app only)
Non-personal Information

We may collect non-personal device and performance information to improve app stability and enhance user experience.

How We Use Your Information
  • To create and manage user accounts
  • To enable secure login and identity verification
  • To process in-app transactions
  • To prevent fraud and unauthorized access
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To send important notifications or updates
How We Protect Your Information

MATRIX uses encryption, secure servers, and strict access controls to protect all personal and financial information. Your data is never shared with third-party marketers.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Only non-identifiable, aggregated usage insights may be used for app performance improvements.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information only if required by law, to comply with legal obligations, or to protect user safety and prevent fraud.

Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your Matrix account from inside the app:

  • Open MATRIX App
  • Go to “Security Settings”
  • Select “Delete Account”
  • Confirm the request

All your personal data, transaction data, and app usage history will be permanently erased.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

MATRIX may update this Privacy Policy when required. Continued use of the app confirms your acceptance of the updated policy.
